# Advisory Frameworks Reference

## Life Coach
- **Wheel of Life**: Rate 8 life areas (career, finance, health, relationships, fun, personal growth, physical environment, contribution) on 1-10 scale to identify imbalances
- **SMART Goals**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- **OKRs**: Objectives & Key Results - ambitious objectives with 3-5 measurable results each
- **Values Clarification**: Identify top 5-7 core values, test decisions against them
- **Ikigai**: Intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, what you can be paid for
- **80/20 Principle**: 20% of activities produce 80% of results - identify and focus on high-leverage actions

## Finance
- **50/30/20 Budget**: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings/debt
- **FIRE**: Financial Independence Retire Early - save 25x annual expenses
- **Asset Allocation**: Diversify across stocks, bonds, real estate, cash based on risk tolerance and time horizon
- **Emergency Fund**: 3-6 months of expenses in liquid savings
- **Debt Avalanche/Snowball**: Pay highest interest first (avalanche) or smallest balance first (snowball)
- **Net Worth Tracking**: Assets minus liabilities, tracked monthly

## Health
- **Exercise Programming**: Progressive overload, periodization, mix of strength + cardio + flexibility
- **Sleep Hygiene**: Consistent schedule, dark/cool room, no screens 1hr before, 7-9 hours
- **Nutrition Fundamentals**: Adequate protein (1.6-2.2g/kg), whole foods, fiber, hydration
- **Stress Management**: Meditation, breathwork, nature exposure, social connection
- **Preventive Health**: Regular checkups, dental, vision, screenings appropriate for age
- **Mental Health**: Therapy awareness, journaling, cognitive reframing, self-compassion

## Career
- **Skills Gap Analysis**: Current skills vs. target role requirements
- **Personal Brand**: Online presence, portfolio, thought leadership
- **Positioning Strategy**: Niche expertise + broad communication skills
- **Network Mapping**: Maintain relationships across industries, mentors, peers, mentees
- **Career Capital Theory** (Cal Newport): Build rare & valuable skills before seeking autonomy
- **SWOT Analysis**: Personal strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats

## Learning
- **Bloom's Taxonomy**: Remember -> Understand -> Apply -> Analyze -> Evaluate -> Create
- **Spaced Repetition**: Review at increasing intervals (1d, 3d, 7d, 14d, 30d, 90d)
- **Deliberate Practice**: Focused practice at the edge of ability with immediate feedback
- **Feynman Technique**: Explain concepts simply to identify gaps in understanding
- **Learning Sprints**: 2-4 week intensive focus on a single skill/topic
- **T-shaped Skills**: Deep expertise in 1-2 areas, broad competence across many

## Systems & Productivity
- **GTD (Getting Things Done)**: Capture -> Clarify -> Organize -> Reflect -> Engage
- **Time Blocking**: Assign specific tasks to calendar blocks, protect deep work
- **Eisenhower Matrix**: Urgent/Important quadrants - focus on Important, Not Urgent
- **Automation Audit**: Identify repetitive tasks that can be automated or eliminated
- **Weekly Review**: Process inbox, review calendar, update goals, plan next week
- **Two-Minute Rule**: If a task takes <2 minutes, do it immediately

## Relationships
- **Dunbar Tiers**: 5 intimate / 15 close / 50 friends / 150 acquaintances
- **Communication Styles**: Direct/indirect, high/low context, assertive/passive
- **Conflict Resolution**: Active listening, I-statements, seek understanding before agreement
- **Social Investment**: Intentional time allocation across relationship tiers
- **Boundaries**: Clear communication of limits, consistent enforcement
- **Love Languages**: Words, acts of service, gifts, quality time, physical touch

## Creative
- **Design Thinking**: Empathize -> Define -> Ideate -> Prototype -> Test
- **Creative Constraints**: Limitations breed creativity - set boundaries to spark ideas
- **Portfolio Management**: Balance exploration (new ideas) with exploitation (finishing projects)
- **Creative Routine**: Consistent time and space for creative work
- **Divergent/Convergent Thinking**: Generate many ideas, then narrow to best ones
- **Feedback Loops**: Share work early and often, iterate based on response
