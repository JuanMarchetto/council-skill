# Assessment Rubrics (1-10 Scale)

Use these rubrics for consistent scoring during reviews. Each domain is rated 1-10 based on the criteria below.

## Life Direction & Purpose (Life Coach)
- **1-2**: No clear direction, feeling lost, no defined values
- **3-4**: Vague sense of direction but no concrete goals, values unclear
- **5-6**: Some goals defined, values partially articulated, moderate alignment between actions and values
- **7-8**: Clear goals with action plans, strong value alignment, meaningful progress
- **9-10**: Deep purpose clarity, goals and values fully integrated, thriving in life direction

## Finance
- **1-2**: No budget, living paycheck to paycheck, growing debt, no savings
- **3-4**: Basic budget awareness, minimal savings, some debt, no investment
- **5-6**: Budget tracked, some savings, debt under control, beginning to invest
- **7-8**: Solid budget discipline, 3-6 month emergency fund, regular investing, clear financial goals
- **9-10**: Financial goals on track, strong net worth growth, diversified investments, on path to financial independence

## Health & Wellness
- **1-2**: Sedentary, poor diet, <6hr sleep, high stress, no medical checkups
- **3-4**: Occasional exercise, inconsistent diet, variable sleep, some stress management
- **5-6**: Regular exercise 2-3x/week, decent diet, 7+ hr sleep most nights, basic stress management
- **7-8**: Consistent exercise 4-5x/week, nutritious diet, good sleep hygiene, effective stress management, regular checkups
- **9-10**: Optimized training program, excellent nutrition, consistent 7-9hr quality sleep, robust mental health practices, all preventive care current

## Career
- **1-2**: Unemployed/severely underemployed, no marketable skills development, no network
- **3-4**: Employed but stagnant, minimal skill growth, weak network
- **5-6**: Stable employment, some skill development, growing network, thinking about career direction
- **7-8**: Good career trajectory, active skill building, strong network, clear development plan
- **9-10**: Thriving career, recognized expertise, extensive network, multiple opportunities, high alignment with values

## Learning & Growth
- **1-2**: No active learning, skill stagnation
- **3-4**: Occasional reading/learning, no structured approach
- **5-6**: Regular learning habits, 1-2 active skill development areas
- **7-8**: Structured learning plan, deliberate practice, measurable skill progress, sharing knowledge
- **9-10**: Continuous deep learning, multiple domains advancing, teaching others, learning systems optimized

## Systems & Productivity
- **1-2**: No task management, constantly reactive, missed deadlines, chaotic workflow
- **3-4**: Basic to-do lists, inconsistent planning, some missed commitments
- **5-6**: Task system in place, weekly planning, meeting most commitments, some automation
- **7-8**: Effective GTD-style system, time blocking, regular reviews, good automation, clear priorities
- **9-10**: Optimized workflows, high automation, consistently deep work, seamless systems, maximum output with minimum friction

## Relationships
- **1-2**: Isolated, no close relationships, frequent conflict, poor communication
- **3-4**: Few close relationships, minimal social investment, some communication issues
- **5-6**: Some good relationships, moderate social life, decent communication
- **7-8**: Strong inner circle, regular social investment, good communication skills, healthy boundaries
- **9-10**: Thriving relationships across all tiers, excellent communication, deep connections, strong community, effective conflict resolution

## Creative
- **1-2**: No creative output, no hobbies, no aesthetic engagement
- **3-4**: Occasional creative activity, inconsistent, no projects completed
- **5-6**: Regular creative time, 1-2 active projects, some creative exploration
- **7-8**: Consistent creative practice, projects completed regularly, experimentation, sharing work
- **9-10**: Prolific creative output, innovative work, multiple completed projects, strong creative identity, inspiring others
